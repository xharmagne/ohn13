Information

OzHadou Nations 13 is the latest iteration of Australia's longest running national fighting games tournament. We are very excited to bring you another three day event filled with lots of games, good times, and everlasting memories.

We will kick things off on Friday from 6pm-11pm for casual gaming and exhibitions designed for players to chill out and have a good time. Players should also take this opportunity to check-in and collect their wristbands. Things get serious on Saturday and Sunday; from 10am-10pm we will be running tournament pools and the finals respectively. If you need more information, check out the schedule below.

Getting there

We will be held in the Red Room at 99onYork (Level 1, 99 York St Sydney). A map has been provided for your convenience below.

Official tournament lineup

We'll be running tournaments for the following games.

DIY tournaments

Due to limited resources and staff, we are only able to cater for the most popular fighting games. In order for other games to obtain national attention and exposure, we offer space for passionate players/organisers who wish to bring their equipment and run tournaments for them.

Space for DIY also has its limits. To make things fair, we will be taking applications up until DD/MM/YYYY and will offer space based on support from the respective communities.

You must purchase a venue entry pass to play any games at OHN and registrations for DIY tournaments will be taken by the DIY organisers on the day of the tournament.

Casual gaming

One of the objectives of OHN is to provide a place for fighting game enthusiasts to gather and share their passion. As such, we will be providing casual play stations throughout the weekend covering all of the games in the official tournament line-up. DIY setups will also be available for casual play outside of tournament hours.

Only registered attendees that have paid the venue entry fee are permitted to play casual games in the OHN venue.

Rules

All official tournaments at OHN are run in accordance with the official OzHadou tournament rules (230kb, PDF). All competitors at OHN are expected to familiarise themselves with these rules before the competition begins.

Please note that these rules can change at any time without prior notice. It is your responsibility to make sure you are up to date with the information that is displayed here.

All tournaments will be Double Elimination, played in 16 player pools. The top 2 players from each of the pools will go into the next round until there are only 8 players remaining. Those players will then compete in the top 8 brackets on the Sunday. Specific rules for each game will be added here shortly.

For game specific rules, please click the tabs for more information.

  • USF4
  • TTT2
  • MKX
Ultra Street Fighter IV

Every match will be best of three (3) games; each game will be best of three (3) rounds with 99 second timer.

The player that loses a game has the option to switch characters for the next game. The winning player has the option to switch ultras.

Tekken Tag Tournament 2

Every match will be best of three (3) games; each game will be best of five (5) rounds with 99 second timer.

The player that loses a game has the option to change their team for the next game.

Mortal Kombat X

Every match will be best of 1 game; each game will be best of 7 rounds with 10 second timer.

The player that loses a game has the option to switch characters for the next game.

Registration

Anyone intending to play games at OHN13 will need to register. Registration will close a few weeks before the event to allow pools and seeding can be finalised. Registering early also helps us immensely by providing realistic numbers.

Registering is super easy; there are 3 simple steps:

  1. Enter your details
  2. Pick the tournaments you're entering (don't forget the competitor's entry!)
  3. Confirm and pay =]

You will get an email confirmation after the transaction has been completed. You will need to bring a copy with you to confirm your registration at the event.

Note: You can pay for multiple people!
